Logging in to the program

The login screen provides you with text fields that allow
you to enter your username and password and you can
click 'Login' to login to the program. Should either your 
username or password be incorrect, the system will 
notify you of the error. Should you have forgotten your 
password then you have to option of clicking the
'Forgot Password' label or creating a new account by 
clicking the 'Create New Account' button. Should your 
login details be correct then you will be shown a welcome
message, the screen will close and the main menu screen 
will be opened.
