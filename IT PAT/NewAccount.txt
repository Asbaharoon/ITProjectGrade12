Creating a New Account

The new account screen will appear when you click the
'Create New Account' button on the login page. This screen
will allow you to enter all your details so as to create your new account. 
You can press 'Create' after you enter your details and if all
the data is valid, you will be asked to enter a security
question as well as the answer to said question. 
Upon the creation of the account, the screen will close and 
the login page will be displayed
